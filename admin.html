Connect Buffer

Buffer is your social media scheduling tool. Connect it here to compose, schedule, and manage posts for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X — without leaving the admin dashboard.

You'll be redirected to Buffer to authorise access. Your Buffer credentials are never stored here.

How to get your Buffer Access Token:
1. Go to buffer.com → Log in → click your avatar → Settings
2. Click Apps & Integrations → Buffer API
3. Click Create an App (or use existing) → copy the Access Token
4. Paste it below, or add it as BUFFER_ACCESS_TOKEN in your Railway environment variables.
